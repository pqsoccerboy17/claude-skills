## Feature Blocks

### 1. Unified Workspace

Stop switching between Slack, email, docs, and project boards. FlowSync pulls everything into a single timeline so nothing slips through the cracks. Every message, file, and update lives where your team already works.

- Real-time activity feed across all channels
- Smart threading that groups related conversations
- Inline document editing without leaving the flow

### 2. Intelligent Prioritization

FlowSync learns what matters most to your team. Its priority engine surfaces blockers, flags overdue items, and nudges the right person at the right time -- without the noise of constant notifications.

- AI-powered task ranking based on team velocity
- Automatic escalation when deadlines are at risk
- Focus mode that silences everything except what needs your attention now

### 3. One-Click Alignment

Weekly status meetings shouldn't take a week to prepare. FlowSync auto-generates project snapshots, decision logs, and progress reports so every stakeholder stays aligned without the meeting tax.

- Auto-generated weekly digests for leadership
