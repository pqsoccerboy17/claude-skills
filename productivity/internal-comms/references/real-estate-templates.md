# Real Estate Communication Templates

Templates for Treehouse LLC tenant and property communications.

## Lease Management

### Lease Renewal Notice (60-day)

```
Subject: Lease Renewal Offer - [Property Address], Unit [#]

Dear [Tenant Name],

Your current lease at [address] is set to expire on [date]. We value you as a tenant and would like to offer you the opportunity to renew.

**Renewal Terms:**
- New lease period: [start date] to [end date]
- Monthly rent: $[amount] (current: $[current amount])
- All other terms remain the same

Please respond by [deadline - 30 days before expiration] to confirm your intentions:
- [ ] I wish to renew my lease
- [ ] I will not be renewing and plan to vacate by [lease end date]

If you choose to renew, I'll send the new lease agreement for your signature.

Please don't hesitate to reach out with any questions.

Best regards,
[Name]
Treehouse LLC
[Phone] | [Email]
```

### Rent Increase Notice

```
Subject: Notice of Rent Adjustment - [Property Address]

Dear [Tenant Name],

**Current monthly rent:** $[current]
**New monthly rent:** $[new]
**Effective date:** [date]

This adjustment reflects [brief explanation: market rates, property improvements, increased operating costs, etc.].

Your updated rent of $[amount] will be due on the first of each month beginning [date].

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact me at your earliest convenience.

Sincerely,
[Name]
Treehouse LLC
```

### Move-In Instructions

```
Subject: Welcome! Move-In Information for [Address]

Dear [Tenant Name],

**Move-In Date:** [Date]
**Key Pickup:** [Location/time]

**Before Move-In:**
- Set up utilities in your name (electric, gas, internet)
  - Electric: [Provider] - [Phone]
  - Gas: [Provider] - [Phone]
- Obtain renter's insurance (required per lease)
- Prepare certified funds for remaining balance: $[amount]

**At Move-In:**
- Complete move-in inspection checklist together
- Receive keys, garage remotes, mailbox keys
- Review emergency contacts and procedures

**Important Contacts:**
- Property Manager: [Name] - [Phone]
- Emergency Maintenance: [Phone]
- Non-emergency Maintenance: [Email/Portal]

**Rent Payment:**
- Amount: $[rent] due on the 1st of each month
- Payment methods: [Zelle/Check/Portal]

Please review the attached move-in checklist before our meeting.

Welcome home!

[Name]
Treehouse LLC
```

### Move-Out Instructions

```
Subject: Move-Out Procedures - [Address]

Dear [Tenant Name],

This letter confirms your move-out date of [date]. Please review the following procedures to ensure a smooth transition and full security deposit return.

**Move-Out Checklist:**
- [ ] Remove all personal belongings
- [ ] Clean all rooms, appliances, and fixtures
- [ ] Patch small nail holes (we'll handle larger repairs)
- [ ] Replace any burnt-out light bulbs
- [ ] Professional carpet cleaning (receipt required)
- [ ] Return all keys, remotes, and access devices
- [ ] Provide forwarding address for deposit return

**Final Walkthrough:**
- Date/Time: [scheduled time]
- Please be present to sign the inspection form

**Utilities:**
- Keep utilities ON through move-out date
- Transfer out of your name effective [date]

**Security Deposit:**
- Will be processed within [X days per state law]
- Mailed to your forwarding address
- Itemized statement if any deductions apply

Questions? Contact me at [phone/email].

Thank you for being a great tenant.

[Name]
Treehouse LLC
```
