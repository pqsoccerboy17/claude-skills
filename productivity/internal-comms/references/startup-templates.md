# Startup Communication Templates

Professional templates for Tap investor relations, team updates, and partnerships.

## Investor Communications

### Monthly Investor Update

```
Subject: Tap [Month] Update - [Headline Metric]

Hi Investors,

Here's your [Month] update for Tap.

**TL;DR:**
[2-3 bullet executive summary]

---

**Key Metrics:**

| Metric | This Month | Last Month | MoM Change |
|--------|------------|------------|------------|
| MRR | $[X] | $[Y] | [+/-]% |
| Active Users | [X] | [Y] | [+/-]% |
| Churn | [X]% | [Y]% | [+/-]% |
| Runway | [X] months | - | - |

**Wins:**
- [Win 1 with context]
- [Win 2 with context]

**Challenges:**
- [Challenge 1 - what we're doing about it]
- [Challenge 2 - what we're doing about it]

**Product:**
- Shipped: [Feature/update]
- In progress: [What's next]

**Team:**
- [Any hires, departures, or updates]

**What's Next:**
- [Priority 1]
- [Priority 2]

**Ask:**
[Specific ask - intros, advice, resources, or "No specific asks this month"]

---

Thanks for your continued support. Happy to discuss any questions.

[Name]
CEO, Tap
```

## Board Communications

### Board Meeting Agenda

```
Subject: Tap Board Meeting - [Date] Agenda

Board Members,

Please find below the agenda for our upcoming board meeting.

**Date:** [Date]
**Time:** [Time] [Timezone]
**Location:** [Address/Video link]

---

**Agenda:**

1. **Call to Order** (5 min)

2. **CEO Update** (20 min)
   - Key metrics review
   - Progress vs. goals
   - Challenges and solutions

3. **Financial Review** (15 min)
   - [CFO/Finance lead]
   - P&L, cash position, forecast

4. **[Topic 1]** (20 min)
   - [Discussion topic requiring board input]

5. **[Topic 2]** (15 min)
   - [Discussion topic]

6. **Closed Session** (15 min)
   - [If applicable]

7. **Action Items & Adjourn** (5 min)

---

**Pre-Read Materials:**
- Board deck (attached)
- Financial model (attached)
- [Other materials]

Please review materials before the meeting.

See you [day].

[Name]
```

### Customer Case Study Request

```
Subject: Share Your Tap Success Story?

Hi [Customer Name],

I hope Tap has been valuable for [their use case]!

We're putting together customer stories and thought [Company] would be a great fit.

**What's Involved:**
- 20-minute interview (can be async via email if preferred)
- Review of draft before publishing
- Opportunity to approve final version

**Benefit for You:**
- Featured on our website/materials
- Social amplification to our audience
- [Any other benefits - credits, swag, etc.]

Would you be open to participating? No pressure either way.

Thanks!
[Name]
```
